About NEW FLYWHEEL

NEW FLYWHEEL is a Solana memecoin that implements the revolutionary Flywheel feature regularly. We collect creator fees from our launchpad Pump.fun and use them to buyback our NEW FLYWHEEL tokens, which are then burned.

This deflationary mechanism increases volume and price while making the coin more attractive to buy and hold as the total supply decreases with each buyback and burn cycle.

How the Flywheel Works

1

Collect Creator Fees

We collect creator fees from Pump.fun launchpad transactions

2

Buyback Tokens

Use collected fees to buyback NEW FLYWHEEL tokens from the market

3

Burn Tokens

Burn the bought tokens, reducing total supply and increasing scarcity

4

Price Appreciation

Reduced supply leads to increased demand and higher token value

Our Story

There was a coin recently called PF Flywheel that implemented the Flywheel mechanism and had initial success, but the developer abandoned the project. The first CTO team also failed badly, leaving many holders disappointed.

We, the second CTO team, decided to take on this challenging mission to do justice for the coin's many holders who were let down twice. We've relaunched with a new name, new smart contract, and significant improvements.

Unfortunately, Pump.fun has not agreed to transfer the creator fees to us, so we've created a fresh start with NEW FLYWHEEL.
